How To Play:

  1. Use the number pad on the keyboard or use your mouse to make your move, with each place on the board corresponding to the layout of the number-pad. For example, the number 1 would mark the bottom left corner of the board.

  2. Players are allowed to undo any move, as long as it's not 'game winning.'

  3. Once one player has three of their marks in a row he/she wins the game, however if there are no more moves to make and nobody has won then the game is a tie.
